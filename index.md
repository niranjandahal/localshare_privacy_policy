# Privacy Policy for LocalShare

**Last Updated**: December 10, 2025

## Overview
LocalShare is a local file sharing application that allows you to transfer files over your local network using SMB (Server Message Block) protocol.

## Data Collection
LocalShare does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All file transfers occur locally on your network.

## Permissions Used

### Internet Permission
Used to establish connections to SMB shared folders on your local network. No data is sent to external servers.

### Storage Permission
Required to:
- Read files from your device for sharing
- Save files received from network shares
- Access and manage files you choose to transfer

### Network Access Permission
Used to discover and connect to SMB/CIFS shared folders on your local WiFi network.

## Data Storage
All data remains on your device or local network. We do not have access to your files or network credentials.

## Third-Party Services
LocalShare does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## What data we collect
- Connection details (IP, username, share name) - stored locally on your device
- No personal data transmitted outside your network

## How we use data
- All data stays on your device and local network
- No analytics or tracking
- No cloud storage

## Permissions explained
- Network access: Required for SMB protocol communication
- File access: Required to download/upload files

## Children's Privacy
LocalShare does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.
